1\. Open your [Phantom](https://phantom.com/) Wallet on your phone.

Click on browser icon -> Type soltap.io in the search box above -> Click on SolTap -> Connect Wallet.

> Only verified tools can be indexed on Phantom Wallet!

2. Now we have successfully connected to the Phantom Wallet, and SolTap has automatically detected how much sol in the wallet can be claimed for you.&#x20;

Click on SolTap -> Confirm.

Congratulations, after just a few simple clicks you have received $SOL, which is the easiest money you can earn.\\
