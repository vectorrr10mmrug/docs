1. Open TradeonNova in Telegram and send command /start.
2. Click on Wallet -> Export Private Key.\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FvfcgIch1zPZ6msM3bXVd%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=25e47237-da5f-46c7-a30d-6687414fcaf3)

3. Select the wallet you want to export. \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FvdVO7HDgf3p5Ma2syVCl%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=e1025826-0ad6-4377-aa21-df08219906e3)

4. Copy your private key\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FiG69zk5mI5v7H8TJSuhh%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=028e12d7-1359-4300-a6e4-6c2241ef27e8)

5. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.
6. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the Nova.
