1. Open [BullX](https://t.me/BullXBetaBot) in Telegram and send command /start.\
   \
   After clicking Login, BullX will automatically open their trading website for you.

![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2F0eqxOWA9qBu9Lkbsz1UA%2Fimage%20(6).avif?alt=media&token=1fa002ba-5bce-488c-88a8-02996332604f)


\\

2. Click the icon in the upper right corner -> Wallet Manager.

3. Click on Wallets -> Select Solana Network -> 2 Private Keys.

![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FWmBCAZXHUnn5225EYpZ5%2Fimage%20(8).png?alt=media&token=6229dd41-229d-4651-88cc-d5dc1b2c4166)


4. Click on Download Private Key. You will download a file containing your wallet address and private key. Copy the private key part.

![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FW3DPqKpqMOoAuXfc67IN%2Fimage%20(9).png?alt=media&token=f71eef03-f217-43d5-b192-1d43f1983425)


5. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.
6. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the BullX.

7. Congratulations, you can now use this wallet to open [soltap.io](https://soltap.io/) to claim free sol. If you need further guidance, you can check out our [tutorial video about phantom wallet.](https://soltap.gitbook.io/docs/how-to-claim-sol/phantom)
