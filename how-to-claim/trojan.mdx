1. Open [Trojan](https://t.me/solana_trojanbot) in Telegram and send command /start.

2. &#x20;Click on Settings > Wallets.

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FKRd9NakGDke4nBAWPgm2%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=f4f1cf07-9124-4656-ae36-8cb5b04f5105)


3. Select the wallet you want to export.

4. Click on Export Private Keys -> Show Private Keys.\\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FkL0VdFVnDoKeAm21oeQK%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=f6eb3c46-c311-4e12-9b27-ed36121fefd1)


5. Copy your Secret Keys (Private Keys).\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2F62MK9w1cFLVnPytybguZ%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=41220a25-924b-49e2-bc76-75139ffd209f)


6. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.\\

7. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the Trojan.
