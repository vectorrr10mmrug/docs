1. Open [Unibo/Unisol](https://t.me/UnisolUnibot) in Telegram and send command /start.

2. Click on Settings -> Export Private Key.\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2F9EJwWxYonm9fZbFJ3K7V%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=7726d7f3-b86d-43ce-b798-80b398e81495)


3. Copy your Private Key.\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FA4ChMCC0DT9hQz7Qriz9%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=3bc4e45f-cacf-41f1-97bb-fa58681cc14e)


4. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.

5. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the Unibot/Unisol.
