1. Open [PepeBoost](https://linktr.ee/pepeboost) in Telegram and send command /start.
2. Click on Wallet -> Export Private Key\
   \\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FtdEJBmONqAw0AuggivOT%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=5a79d4ad-c79e-427d-ace8-1c41b7647688)

3. Copy the private key part.

![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2F0syqMI9QCqWMY90D6FLE%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=fc5f32d4-c4e7-4cbf-a5ac-c01a4682e3f5)


3. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.
4. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the PepeBoost.
