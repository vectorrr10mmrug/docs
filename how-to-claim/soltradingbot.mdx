1. Open [SOLTradingBot ](https://t.me/SolTradingBot)in Telegram and send command /start.

2. Click on Wallets -> Select the wallet you want to export.

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2F6BmxT2etM5XS372mnkdy%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=8d8b2d45-3ae9-4684-ac8a-4887a244e280)


3. Click on the privacy mask -> Copy your Private Key.

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FHsiC2QunTSBgyrtNnrws%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=d97dbe04-92fc-44bb-b2bc-e2cb837dde70)


4. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.

5. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the SOLTradingBot.
