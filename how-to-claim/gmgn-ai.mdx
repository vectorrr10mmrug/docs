1. Open [GMGN.AI Telegram Trading Bot](https://t.me/gmgnaibot) in Telegram and send command /start.
2. Click on Login Website.\\

   ![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FyaiHl2MKvh6NHa2SJ4BR%2Fimage.png?alt=media&token=92eb06f2-2601-45e3-8d52-0e0d30c16c15)

3. Click on the account management area in the upper right corner -> Import or Export Private Key.\
   \
   This will redirect you to the [GMGN Sniper Bot.](https://t.me/GMGN_sol_bot?start=wallet)

![](https://2619786285-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FM0pXZISmirZR5oaAIkSt%2Fuploads%2FJO6zgCQXeB5c3xahmtJM%2Fimage%20(7).avif?alt=media&token=ed20ddc6-3271-488e-aa63-64ed9e775cd5)


4. Now we are in GMGN Sniper Bot.\
   \
   Click on Export Private Key -> Copy your Private Key.\\

5. Open your phantom wallet on your phone and click the icon in the upper left corner.
6. Click on Add/Connect Wallet -> Import Private Key, and paste the private key you copied on the GMGN.AI
